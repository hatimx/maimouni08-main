Delivery Information

discover our Delivery Information

At Maimouni 08, we are committed to delivering your orders in a convenient and timely manner. Please review the following shipping details:

Shipping to Morocco and Worldwide

We proudly offer shipping services to customers both in Morocco and worldwide. Whether you're located in Morocco or any other corner of the globe, we're here to serve you.

Estimated Delivery Times

Our delivery times may vary depending on the type of product and your location:
Morocco: Within Morocco, most orders are typically delivered within 7 days from the date of purchase.
International: For international deliveries, please note that shipping times may vary depending on your location and any customs or import regulations in your country. We aim to deliver your order as efficiently as possible.

Delivery Updates

Once your order is processed and shipped, you will receive an email notification containing a tracking code. This code will allow you to track the status and location of your shipment on our chosen courier's website.

Returns Policy

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you wish to return an item, please review our Returns Policy:
Returns are accepted within 14 days of the delivery date, provided the item is in its original condition and packaging.
To initiate a return, please Contact Us and our team will guide you through the process.

Important Notes

Please note that some specialized or bespoke items may have longer delivery times. We recommend confirming the estimated delivery time with our sales team before placing your order.
The shipping cost for each order is determined based on the destination. Additional charges may apply for non-standard delivery destinations.
Please ensure you inspect your item upon delivery. If the item is damaged in any way, it must be signed for as "DAMAGED," or the delivery may be refused. Any issues with the item should be reported within 24 hours.

Changes to This Agreement

We reserve the right to modify or replace these Terms and Conditions by posting the updated terms on the Website. Your continued use of the Website after any such changes constitutes your acceptance of the new Terms and Conditions.
